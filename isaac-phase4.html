BAR EXPERIENCE DESIGN PRINCIPLES
📱 Phone is the controller
Pick on your phone. Results on your phone. But your name appears on the big screen — creating public stakes that make the game more thrilling and make others want in.
🍺 Bar Battle is the hook
Not just IQ points — a real social prize at the bar. Top scorer wins a round, a discount, recognition. Tangible reward makes every pick feel higher stakes.
📺 TV creates FOMO
Non-players watch the bar TV, see friends winning, see the leaderboard — and want in. The bar TV is SportsIQ's best acquisition tool. Every bar night creates new users.
⚡ Glanceable UI
Bar environment: loud, distracted, beer in one hand. Every screen optimized for 2-second glances. Huge numbers, minimal text, clear color coding (green = winning).
🔄 Loss = immediate next pick
Losses at the bar are the highest-risk churn moment. Redemption pick CTA fires instantly: "Win this → jump from #4 to #2." No wallowing — always a path back.
📅 End of night = next visit hook
Final standings fire a "Rematch?" challenge and a "Defend your title Thursday" reminder. Turns a one-time bar visit into a recurring weekly ritual.
