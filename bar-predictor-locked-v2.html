Design Mechanics · Bar predictor locked
Top bar — LIVE → 🔒 LOCKED badge (green). Same mode badge (Predictor). 3× perk unchanged.
YES/NO pick buttons removed — voting is closed. Replaced by the Tracking card. This is the signal that patrons can no longer scan to join this particular pick (but QR still works for next question and trivia).
Tracking card — green, glowing, dominates the center. "Tracking Live" label with pulsing dot. Big stat number (0) + label ("LeBron points in Q3 so far"). Updates live as the game unfolds — turns green when prediction becomes likely, flashes red as it slips.
Resolution countdown — "Resolves at Q3 end · 6:42" anchors anticipation. Patrons know exactly when the outcome locks.
Crowd split revealed — now that voting is done, showing "Room picked 66% YES · 34% NO" no longer causes herd bias. It creates shared drama: "most of us picked YES, if LeBron scores we all win together."
Sharp $ stays visible — external market consensus for comparison. As game unfolds, Sharp $ may drift (odds shift live) — shown with "updates live" caption.
Bottom strip — counts switch from "picking" to "locked in" — 1,284 nationally · 38 at Kincade's.
