Design Mechanics · Bar trivia reveal
Ambient glow shifts green — the whole frame softly washes green on a correct-answer reveal. Subtle, not overwhelming, just enough for the crowd to feel "that was the answer."
LIVE badge → REVEAL badge — green check + "REVEAL" replaces the red LIVE badge. Still pulses for attention but signals "outcome is in."
Correct answer lights up — Answer A (Michael Jordan) glows green with filled gradient, green letter bubble, big green ✓ checkmark top-right. Impossible to miss across the bar.
Percentages appear on every answer — 43% · 28% · 19% · 10%. Thin filled bar below each shows the proportion visually. Wrong answers dim to ~50% opacity so the correct one dominates attention.
Same percentages as mobile reveal — synchronized. What mobile players see in their reveal-v2 state, bar screen shows identically on the TV.
Left panel "CURRENT GAME" updates live — JessB and RaviP just got this Q right, both jumped to +250 with a green "+100" delta badge. Viewers SEE the leaderboard move.
Fun fact below answers — same copy as mobile, sized bigger for TV. Gives viewers a reason to keep watching even if they got it wrong.
Auto-advance — "Next in 0:02" in the top row. 2-second reveal then auto-advances to next Q. Same cadence as mobile.
Bottom strip localizes — "3,847 answered this Q nationally · 32 got it right at Kincade's" frames the bar's performance against the country.
