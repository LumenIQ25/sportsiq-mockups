Design Mechanics
Celebration aesthetic — subtle green ambient glow at top, small confetti particles. Reads as "you won" without being aggressive.
Hero score ring — conic gradient filled to 70% (7/10), green glow, big tabular number. Draws the eye first.
3 stat cards — IQ (amber) · Rank (green, with ↑) · Streak (orange). Each color-coded to its vibe.
Affiliate handoff — contextual to category performance. "Hot on NBA" + "Lakers tip off in 18 min" + "Put real money on your next call?" arrow. Only shows when user scored well in a category with a live game available.
Leaderboard preview — 2 rows: the top player (gold), and you (purple highlighted). Tap for full board.
Primary CTA — "Next Round · 2:43" is the default action. Keeps user in the loop. Share is secondary.
State variants not shown — weak result (<5/10) skips affiliate card, replaces celebration with redemption framing ("Next round in 2:43 — redeem yourself"). Perfect round (10/10) gets stronger celebration, unlocks Sharp Badge.
