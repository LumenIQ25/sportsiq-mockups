Design Mechanics
Focused pill (🧠 Trivia) — filled with brand gradient, elevated shadow, brighter timer color.
Background pills (🏀 LAL, 🎯 Bingo) — outlined only, muted text.
Pulse dot on Bingo — newly-started event signal.
Interactions — tap any pill to swap hero focus; swipe left/right on hero = same.
Urgency — timer <10 sec triggers red flash on that pill.
Never interrupts — new events slide in with a pulse; no modals.
State preservation — swap out, swap back = exactly where you left off.
